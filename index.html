Our story

Made by opticians,
not algorithms.

Aether NY began in a small workshop with a stubborn idea: that buying glasses online shouldn’t mean guessing. Every frame is cut from Italian acetate, tumbled for a week to a soft shine, and finished by hand.

 

The try-on came later — our answer to the fitting-room problem. Measure once, see everything, and only order the pair that’s truly yours.
